Frequently Asked Questions

QUESTIONS ABOUT BOOKING AND PAYMENT

CAN I ONLY BOOK AN APPOINTMENT ONLINE?▼

Of course, you can also book on-site. However, we cannot guarantee availability of space and firearms on the same day, so it is recommended to book in advance on the website.

CAN I PAY ON-SITE?▼

Absolutely! In addition to transferring the amount to the account number provided on the website, you can pay on-site with cash, a bank card, or a SZÉP card.

HOW MANY PEOPLE CAN I BOOK FOR AT MOST?▼

Typically, a group consists of up to 7 people. If you'd like to book for more, please send an email with the details. Additionally, for groups larger than one person, include the names of participants and the chosen package in the 'Other Notes' section.

CAN I MODIFY MY APPOINTMENT AFTER BOOKING?▼

Yes, it is possible. You can modify your appointment via email up to 72 hours before the scheduled time.

IS IT POSSIBLE FOR SOMEONE ELSE TO GO IN MY PLACE, EVEN IF THE RESERVATION IS UNDER MY NAME?▼

This is a more detailed description number 3 that appears after clicking.

IS SHOOTING CONDUCTED IN GROUPS?▼

Yes, shooting is conducted in groups of up to 7 people, but sessions can also be arranged for just one person.

IS PRIVATE SHOOTING AVAILABLE?▼

Yes, it is available, but it comes with higher costs. For details, please inquire via email.

QUESTIONS ABOUT PACKAGES

IS IT MANDATORY TO CHOOSE A PACKAGE?▼

No, but in this case, you need to select the "Optional Package" option and specify in the notes section which firearms you would like to use.

CAN MEMBERS OF THE SAME GROUP CHOOSE DIFFERENT PACKAGES?▼

Absolutely! In this case, please specify in the notes section which packages you would like to use.

I ONLY WANT TO TRY ONE FIREARM. IS THAT POSSIBLE?▼

Yes, it is possible. However, you need to arrange an appointment by phone, and private shooting rates will apply.

AGE RESTRICTIONS

HOW OLD DO I HAVE TO BE TO PARTICIPATE IN EXPERIENCE SHOOTING?▼

Only individuals who are legally capable and have reached the age of 18 may participate.

CAN I ATTEND AS A SPECTATOR IF I'M UNDER 18?▼

Yes, you can attend as a spectator, but you must be at least 14 years old and have at least one parent or guardian present.

CAN I PARTICIPATE IN EXPERIENCE SHOOTING IF I'M UNDER 14?▼

Individuals who have not yet reached the age of 14 may not participate in experience shooting under any circumstances.

SHOOTING RANGE RULES

DO I NEED ANY PRIOR EXPERIENCE, OR CAN I COME AS A BEGINNER?▼

No prior experience is necessary. Most of our guests are beginners, and we are prepared for that in every session.

DO YOU PROVIDE TRAINING BEFORE SHOOTING?▼

Yes, we conduct training before every session to ensure your safety and the safety of others.

WHAT KIND OF CLOTHING SHOULD I WEAR?▼

Wear comfortable clothing that you don't mind getting dirty. As for footwear, it should be comfortable and closed.

IS EXPERIENCE SHOOTING DANGEROUS?▼

It is safe as long as all safety regulations are followed.

DO I NEED TO BRING MY OWN PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT?▼

No, we provide all necessary tools and protective equipment.
